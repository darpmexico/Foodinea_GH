Frequently Asked Questions

What is Foodinea?

Foodinea is the Latin America leader platform to book tours and activities for Foodies platform. With us, you can book tours and activities in advance: this way you get the best prices and you can plan your gastronomic adventure with the advantage of not needing to deal with cash once you're in your destination. Your mission: Enjoy your adventure through your palate.

What types of tours and activities are offered on Foodinea?

We offer tours and activities for foodies, wine lovers and culinary adventurers.Enjoy authentic Mexican tacos, eat barbecue as Argentinian, or enjoy a gourmet dinner at the Chef´s House. In Foodinea We have over 400 authentic dining experiences in Mexico, Chile and Argentina to make your trip a memorable one. Expect our next destinations; so book a tour, and discover Latin America eating like a local.

Are tours offered only in English?

Our guides are bilingual locals who speak fluent English. Remember that you can ask your tour in Spanish, ask for it to guide.

How do I book a foodie experience?

If you know what type of activity you are looking for, enter that word in the search bar at the top of the page Foodinea. Remember that you can search by country, city or category of activity.

Book your adventure through Foodinea is easy. Simply choose the date you want to book, indicating the number of people who participate in the tour, select the option book.

Then choose your payment method, fill the form, you receive your confirmation email and get ready for adventure.

What are the payment methods?

We remind you that your payment is secure, your information will be encrypted to protect against fraud.

Payment options:

  • Credit Card.
  • Debit Card
  • Wire transfer *

* Send an email to hola@foodinea.com to know the account number.

Once your tour has been confirmed you will receive a confirmation email with attached invoice and any additional information needed about your adventure.

How do I ask a question about an activity before making a booking?

If you have specific question about an activity please email your query to hola@foodinea.com, we will respond as soon as possible.

What happens if I want to cancel a booking?

If you need to cancel your reservation no problem. The following cancellation policies apply to all bookings confirmed by Foodinea:

  • Up to 5 days before the reservation: 100% refund.
  • 24 hours to 4 days before the reservation canceling: 50% refund.
  • Within 24 hours before starting your activity or if you do not go: No refund
